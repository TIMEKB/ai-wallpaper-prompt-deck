Shike AI Wallpaper — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 10 July 2026 · Contact: zzqtim@gmail.com

Shike AI Wallpaper ("the app", "we") is an Android app that generates AI wallpapers from prompt cards you choose and sets them as your Home or Lock screen. This policy explains what data we process, why, and the choices you have. If you have any questions, contact us at zzqtim@gmail.com.

Data we process

DataWhyShared with
Your prompts (the tags/text you select to generate an image)To generate a wallpaperThe AI image generation service that fulfils the request
A hashed device identifier (a one-way hash of a device ID)Free daily quota, membership entitlement, and abuse preventionOur own backend only
Google Play purchase token and subscription stateTo verify and grant your subscriptionGoogle Play; our own backend
Content reports (image ID, the prompt, reason, and hashed device ID)To review reports of unsafe AI-generated content and keep the app safeOur own backend only
Advertising IDTo show ads to non-subscribers (subscribers are ad-free)Google AdMob

Your generated images, history, favorites, ratings and settings are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers. The images themselves are not sent with a content report — only the prompt and metadata needed for review.

Who processes data for us

  • AI image generation provider — receives your prompt to generate the wallpaper.
  • Google Play Billing — processes subscription purchases; we verify the purchase token.
  • Google AdMob — serves ads and uses the advertising ID. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — hosts our backend (quota, membership verification, content reports).

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention and deletion

Local data remains until you clear the app's data or uninstall the app. Membership and report records tied to your hashed device identifier are kept only as long as needed for the service or as required by law. To request deletion of data associated with your device, email zzqtim@gmail.com.

Security

All network traffic uses HTTPS. Membership tokens are verified with an offline signature and stored encrypted on your device.

Children

The app is intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International transfers

Because the services above operate globally, your data may be processed in countries other than your own, with appropriate safeguards.

Your choices

You can clear all local data via your system settings ("Clear data") or by uninstalling the app. Subscribers do not see ads. You can manage or cancel your subscription in Google Play.

Changes

We may update this policy; significant changes will be indicated in the app or on this page.

Contact

Questions, requests or complaints: zzqtim@gmail.com
